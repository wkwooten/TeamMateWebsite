Find Your TeamMate

Finding someone who share’s mutual passions can be a real challenge in today’s dating scene.

Sports can create meaningful connections across the globe, so we thought we would create a safe space where individuals can foster relationships over these shared passions. Whether its connecting with other super fans of the same team or embracing cross-town rivals, Team Mate wants to connect you with others that bleed blue, red, orange, green, and purple.

Connect, Chat, and if things go well, you can purchase tickets to a local event for a memorable first date.

Sporting Events can represent a safe public space where you can show your true colors for the team you love and in doing so maybe find your Team Mate for life!
