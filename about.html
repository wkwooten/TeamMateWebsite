Kick Off the Connection

“In the game of hearts, every move is a risk worth taking. You feel the rush, like the final sprint to the finish line - unstoppable, breathless, and craving the next play.”

How it Works:

1. Create Your Profile

After entering some details about yourself, you can select your favorite sports and favorite teams. We offer inclusive options so that everyone can play.

2. Match and Chat

Once a match is made, you’re able to chat over recent headlines, box scores, and whatever else your hearts desire.

3. Schedule a Date

If things go well, you can purchase tickets to a local game and meet up to watch your favorite team play!

Too high stakes for you? We also offer the option to schedule a date at a local restaurant.

Connection is the Goal

At Team Mate the only focus has been on building an app that allows people to showcase their passion for sports. Our fandoms can become religion in some places and who you root for matters almost as much as who you vote for.

Connecting These Passions:

It’s these passions that Team Mate hopes to bring out in people and allow them to connect with others who share in the fervor and find attraction the the passion.
