Connection is the Goal

At Team Mate the only focus has been on building an app that allows people to showcase a piece of what makes them tick. Our fandoms can become religion in some places and who you root for matters almost as much as who you vote for.

How it Works

After creating your profile, select your favorite sports and teams, and browse similar-minded fans.

Match and Chat

Once a match is made, you’re able to chat over recent headlines, box scores, and more.

Plan a Game Day
