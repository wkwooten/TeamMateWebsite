Playbook Rules

We believe in a safe, inclusive, and equitable environment where everyone can play safe. We hope you can keep up with the playbook.

🏆 Play with Integrity

Keep your profile true to who you are—accurate photos, age, and bio. Authenticity wins.

🛡️ Stay in Defense Mode

Guard your personal info. Take your time and play it safe when connecting.

🤝 Keep it Sportsmanlike

Respect your fellow players. Treat others the way you want to be treated, both on and off the field.

🚩 Call Out the Fouls

Help keep the game fair. Report any bad behavior you encounter.
